This website is a reference for offroad trails in Tennessee and surrounding areas.

Name Difficulty Description Location Cost
Franklin State Forest Beginner Trails scattered through a TN state park are friendly for ATVs and SxS. Sherwood, TN Free
Coalmont OHV Park Intermediate Rocky trails wind around the mountain and into the hollow where a creek runs. There is a waterfall. Coalmont, TN $20 for parking
Adventure Offroad Park Advanced This site is best suited for rock crawlers. Some trails are suitable for ATVs/SxS. South Pittsburg, TN $20
Circle E Guest Ranch Advanced Large park with two alternating, rideable sides. There is a scenic waterfall. Belvidere, TN $20
Windrock Intermediate Fully equipped offroad and camping park well known to offroad riders in the southeast. Oliver Springs, TN $27.33
Pickett State Forest OHV Area Intermediate State Park near the Kentucky border with many hours of riding and camping nearby. Jamestown, TN Free
Brimstone Recreation Intermediate Access to 300 miles of OHV trails, lodging, camping, and events. Huntsville, TN $28 per adult
Turkey Bay OHV Area Intermediate Multiple marked trails over 100s of acres at Land Between The Lakes in Kentucky. Murray, KY $20
Royal Blue Resort Intermediate Great riding with campgrounds and other amenities for all. Pioneer, TN Variable
